Our Campuses

Siddhartha Institute of Science and Technology

Siddhartha Institute of Science and Technology (SISTK), established in 2008, is a premier engineering institution located in Puttur, Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh. Nestled on an 11-acre campus amidst the scenic Eastern Ghats, the college is situated near Narayanavanam, a revered site where Lord Venkateswara is believed to have married Goddess Padmavathi. Its strategic location offers excellent connectivity, being just 22 km from Tirupati International Airport and well-linked by rail and road networks .

Our Facilites

World Class Library

The Central Library at Siddharth Institute of Engineering and Technology (SIETK), Puttur, serves as a cornerstone of academic excellence, providing a comprehensive and modern learning environment for students and faculty alike.

Foot Ball Court

The Siddharth Institute of Engineering and Technology (SIETK), Puttur, places a strong emphasis on the overall development of its students, including physical fitness and extracurricular engagement. The college campus features a well-maintained football court that serves as a key facility for sports and recreation.

World Class Hostel Facility

Siddharth Institute of Engineering and Technology (SIETK) in Puttur offers comprehensive hostel facilities designed to provide a comfortable and secure living environment for its students. The institute has four hostels: two for boys and two for girls, ensuring separate and secure accommodations for all students. Each hostel is equipped with essential amenities to cater to the daily needs of the residents
