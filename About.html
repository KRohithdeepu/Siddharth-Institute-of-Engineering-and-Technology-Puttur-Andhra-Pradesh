About Us

Established in 2001 under the aegis of Jaya Educational Society, Siddharth Institute of Engineering and Technology (SIETK) is a premier autonomous institution located in Puttur, Andhra Pradesh. Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur (JNTUA), and approved by AICTE, SIETK has earned NBA accreditation for five programs and an 'A+' grade from NAAC, reflecting its commitment to academic excellence. Situated on a 30-acre campus amidst the scenic Eastern Ghats, SIETK offers a serene and conducive environment for learning. The institute provides a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs in engineering and management disciplines. With a focus on holistic development, SIETK emphasizes innovative teaching methodologies, industry collaborations, and research initiatives. The campus boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern laboratories, digital classrooms, a comprehensive library, and dedicated centers of excellence in partnership with leading global companies like Microsoft, IBM, Intel, and Siemens . SIETK College Under the visionary leadership of Chairman Dr. K. Ashok Raju, SIETK has become a nurturing ground for aspiring engineers and managers, particularly from rural backgrounds. The institute's mission is to produce ethically strong and technically proficient professionals who can contribute meaningfully to society .
